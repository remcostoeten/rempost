My recommendation: A

For "where is my package", one screen is the right answer. Autocomplete makes typos a non-issue. B's "recent" list is tempting but only helps the first friend who looks today — and it shows everyone's orders to everyone (you said that's fine, but it's not actually useful UX). C adds friction in the name of warmth; a clean single field with a nice greeting on top achieves the same warmth without the extra screens.
